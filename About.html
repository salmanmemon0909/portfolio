I’m Salman Memon, but you can just call me Salman.

I'm from Pakistan and I've been working in design, production and management roles
building products for the web, mobile, games and, more recently, A.I & ML.

I strive to create elegant solutions that surprise and delight users, while keeping
complex technical dependencies in mind for implementation, scalability and
developer sanity.

I’ve worked with small, agile teams on skunkworks projects and larger development
teams with product lifecycles spanning multiple years.

Depending on the team, resources and deliverables I’ve worn many hats over the
years - holding titles like Product Designer, UI Designer, UX Designer, Front-end
Developer, Growth Lead, Producer, Product Manager & Product Owner.

I also occasionally give talks on design and product development.
